The BIOMASS mission will deliver a suite of <b>global, consistent forest products</b> derived from P-band SAR observations. Core data products include <b>above-ground forest biomass</b>, <b>forest height</b>, and <b>forest disturbance</b>, providing unprecedented insight into forest structure, carbon stocks, and their changes over time. These products are generated as Level-2 and Level-3 datasets, designed for long-term monitoring, climate studies, and Earth system modelling.

In addition to the core products, BIOMASS will produce <b>experimental and exploratory datasets</b> that exploit the unique capabilities of P-band SAR, supporting advanced research on forest dynamics, subsurface scattering, and ecosystem processes. All products are delivered with uncertainty information and follow open-science principles to maximise scientific and societal impact.

## <p style={{ fontVariant: 'small-caps' }}>Open Access, Open Science, and Community Engagement</p>

All BIOMASS products, algorithms, and validation tools are developed following open science and open-source principles. Processing software and algorithm development are hosted within the BIOMASS Product Algorithm Laboratory (<b>BioPAL</b>), enabling transparency, reproducibility, and community-driven innovation. Open access to data, processing environments, and analysis tools is further supported through the Multi-mission Algorithm and Analysis Platform (<b>MAAP</b>), which facilitates collaborative research, reproducible workflows, and cross-mission scientific exploitation.

🔗 MAAP: <a target='blank' style={{color: "var(--accent-1)"}} href='https://maap-project.org'>https://maap-project.org</a>

## <p style={{ fontVariant: 'small-caps' }}>BIOMASS Data Innovation and Science Cluster (DISC)  </p>

<p><WP number={1}>Key Activities</WP></p>

The BIOMASS DISC ensures the quality, reliability, and scientific value of BIOMASS mission products. Its main activities include:
<p className="leading-tight">
<b> Instrument and Data Quality Monitoring</b>  <br />
Continuous calibration, performance monitoring, and quality control to ensure accurate and reliable BIOMASS SAR data.  

<b>Processing and Product Evolution</b>  
Maintenance and improvement of processing chains and core products, integrating scientific advances, calibration, and validation results.  

<b>Validation and Reprocessing</b>  
Validation of forest products against high-quality ground and airborne reference data, and support for regular reprocessing to maintain consistent long-term data records.  

<b>Scientific Innovation and User Support</b>  
Development of experimental products, support to the user community, and extension of BIOMASS scientific capabilities.  
</p>
Further information: <a style={{color: "var(--accent-1)"}} href='https://biomass-disc.info/'>https://biomass-disc.info/</a>

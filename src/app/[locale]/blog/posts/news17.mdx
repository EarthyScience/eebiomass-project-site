It is time ! On Tuesday April 29th, 2025, at 6:15 a.m. local time (09:15 a.m. UTC, 11:15 a.m. CEST), Earth Explorer BIOMASS will be launched from Europe’s Spaceport in French Guiana.

Watch the event live here: <a style={{color: "var(--accent-1)"}} href='https://www.esa.int/ESA_Multimedia/ESA_Web_TV'>https://www.esa.int/ESA_Multimedia/ESA_Web_TV</a> starting 10:45 CEST

11:15 Biomass launches on Vega-C
12:13 Separation and placement of Biomass in orbit
12:27 Acquisition of signal
12:38 End of launch broadcast

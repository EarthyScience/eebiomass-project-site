Partners from across Europe came together from 2nd to 5th June, 2025, in Lund for the first AI4PEX General Assembly — a pivotal moment in our mission to push the boundaries of #EarthSystemModels with cutting-edge #EarthObservation, #MachineLearning and #AI.
Hosted by @lunduniversity, the four days were packed with:
✨ Shaping benchmarking tools — tackling methodological challenges and cross-model needs.
🛠 Reviewing progress & syncing milestones — taking stock and refining our shared roadmap.
🌍 Boosting outreach & community building — strengthening our impact strategy and stakeholder networks.
🤝 Hands-on collaboration — planning the next six months of focused work in dedicated groups.



The spirit of collaboration and innovation is already propelling AI4PEX forward — laying the groundwork for new insights into climate and Earth system dynamics..

EEBIOMASS Summer School

EEBIOMASS summer schools are aimed to engage and train Early Career scientists on different dimensions of biomass and Earth observation related topics! Target group of the Summer School were Early Careers from the fields of Remote Sensing, Forest Management and Earth System Sciences.

The Project Office BIOMASS and the Coordinated German Contribution to the BIOMASS Cal/Val Activities (DE:CalVal) are funded by the German Space Agency (DLR) with funds from the Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Climate Action (BMWK) under grant no. 50EE1904 & 50EE2514.

# EEBIOMASS project site

This repository holds the public website for **EEBIOMASS** — the German **Project Office BIOMASS**, which works with the national community around ESA’s **Earth Explorer BIOMASS** mission (P-band SAR for forest biomass and the carbon cycle). The office is a cooperation of MPI for Biogeochemistry, Friedrich Schiller University Jena, DLR, and UFZ. The live site is at [eebiomass.org](https://www.eebiomass.org/).

## What’s on the site

**Project Office BIOMASS** — Background on the office, who is involved, how work is organised (work packages), and project-related publications.

**German contribution to BIOMASS Cal/Val** — Information on calibration and validation activities: overview and team.

**BIOMASS mission** — Mission context: what BIOMASS is, how it fits among ESA Earth Explorers and related programmes, and pointers to data and the Data Innovation and Science Cluster (DISC) where relevant.

**News** — Updates and announcements.

**Events** — Webinars, summer schools, and the PolInSAR course line supported in connection with the project.

**Resources** — Further reading, related missions and projects, teaching materials and tools, and the newsletter.

**Contact** — How to reach the project office. **Imprint** and **privacy** pages cover legal notices for the site.
